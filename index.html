About

Join me at rubyonrailstutor.com for a social pair programming boot-camp. We will explore the fundamentals of full stack web application programming and master the basics of:

  • ruby
  • rails
  • test driven development
  • sublime
  • git & github
  • haml
  • heroku
  • responsive web design

Prior to 2012, I didn't know how to build software. Thanks to Dev Bootcamp and a ton of hard work and support from the San Francisco developer community, in the span of 3 rigorous training months and 18 months working, I worked with 4 startups, 1 consultancy, 1 publicly traded corporation, co-built crowdsound.io, built numerous hackathon level products and helped many people get on the path to professional software development.

I look forward to guiding you through the same tranformation.

John Davison, @railstutor
