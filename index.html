Join me at rubyonrailstutor.com for a fun highly social, distributed real time pair programming class series where we will explore the fundamantels of web application development. Thanks to Dev Bootcamp and a ton of hard work and support from the San Francisco developer community, in the span of 3 rigorous training months and 18 months working, I now operate as a wholly independent software product tactition.
